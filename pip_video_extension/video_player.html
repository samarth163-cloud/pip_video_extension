About Auto-Play Companion

Browsers normally restrict video playback without user interaction. Once you click Play once, the extension handles the rest:

  • Entering PiP: Keeps video playing inside the floating PiP window.
  • Returning to Page: Keeps video playing inline on the page.
  • Tab Switching: (Global feature) Switches video to PiP when leaving, and restores it when returning.
